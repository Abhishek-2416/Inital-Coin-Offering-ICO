                                                        ICO (Initial Coin Offering)
    1. An ICO is or Inital Coin offering is a type of crowdfuding using cryptocurrencies

    2. An ICO can be a souce of capital for startup companies that offer investors some units of a new cryptocurrencies or crypto tokens
       in exchange for a well known and valuable cryptocurrency like ethereum

    3. Our ICO will be a smart contract that accepts ETH in exchange for our own token named CRYPTOS(CRPT)

    4. The Cryptos token is a fully complaint ERC20 token which will be generated at the ICO time 

    5. Investors will send ETH to the ICO contract address and in return they will get an amount of Cryptos

    6. There will be a deposit address (EOA account) that automatically receives the ETH sent to the ICO contract

    7. CRPT token price will be 1CRPT = 0.001ETH or 1 ETH = 1000CRPT

    8. The minimum incestment is 0.01 ETH and the maximum investment is 5ETH

    9. The ICO hardcap is 300ETH

    10. The ICO will have and admin that specifies when the ICO starts and ends 

    11. The ICO ends when the hardcap has been reached or the end time has reached 

    12. The CRPT will be traded only after a specific date which is set by the admin

    13. In case of emergency the admin could stop the ICO and could also change the deposit address in case it gets compramised

    14. The ICO can be in one of the following states beforeStart , runnning , afterEnd, halted

    15. And we will also implement a feature to burn the token which were not sold in the ICO 

    16. After an investment in the ICO the invest event will be emitted
